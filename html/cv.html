Pharaz Azimi's work embraces abstract and metaphoric visual language based on the social-political, cultural and self expression of his surroundings and experiences. Through my installations, I express my feelings and impressions referring to architecture, daily life objects, literature, and philosophy. It is a continuing process of researching content, forms, and techniques leading me to my final works, where I connect my ideas and interests in materials such as wood, metal, plexiglas, ceramic, plaster, and foams.
