Pharaz Azimi's work embraces abstract and metaphoric visual language based on the social-political, cultural and self expression of his surroundings and experiences. Through his installations, he expresss his feelings and impressions referring to architecture, daily life objects, literature, and philosophy. It is a continuing process of researching content, forms, and techniques leading him to the final works, where he connects his ideas and interests in materials such as wood, metal, plexiglas, ceramic, plaster, and foams.
